Dr. Strange Quest

Dr. Strange is so over powered on current situation that he can defeat everyone so fast with better obelisk(damage increase). He can freeze time, it means if this happends you can't do anything except to see him to reduce your character's hit point.

To get Dr. Strange fully ranked i.e. 6* you need to accomplish some quest which is called "Epic Quest".

There are total 45 quest. And some quest gives you good rewards like Crystals, Comic Card, Obelisk etc. Through this quests you will get him 6*. After completing some quests it will unlock two missions "Memory Mission" & "Dark Mission".

When you open your Epic Question it will show you this menu. There are four options:

Story: It shows you your achievement and what you need to complete.

Research: where you need to use some resources like Norn Stones, Black Anti Matter, Chaotic Norn Stones etc. to rank and mastery up Dr. Strange.

Memory Mission: On memory mission there are four sub missions: Road to the Monastery, Mysterious Ambush, Monastery in Trouble & Power of the Dark.

Dark Mission has two sub mission part, one is Dark Advent and another one is Increasing Darkness

Every day you will be given 10 entry to complete missions. When you complete certain quests you will be given first sub mission which is Road to the monastery and rest will be locked until you finish/complete your quests.

Different missions will drop different character bios:

Road to the Monastery: Biometrics: Baron Mordo, LVL4 Exp Chip/Gear Up Kit/Custom Gear/Obelisk/High Obelisk

Mysterious Ambush: Biometrics: Wong, LVL4 Exp Chip/Gear Up Kit/Custom Gear/Obelisk/High Obelisk

Mysterious Ambush: Biometrics: Ancient One, LVL4 Exp Chip/Gear Up Kit/Custom Gear/Obelisk/High Obelisk

Power of the Dark: Biometrics: Kaecilius, LVL4 Exp Chip/Gear Up Kit/Custom Gear/Obelisk/High Obelisk

Every day you will be given 10 entry to complete dark missions. When you complete certain quests you will be given first sub mission which is Dark Advent and another will be locked until you finish/complete your quests.

Different missions will drop different character bios:

Dark Advent: Biometrics: Satana, different types of 3 star ISO

Increasing Darkness: Biometrics: Hellstorm, different types of 3 star ISO

Missions

Story Mission

There are 10 Chapters for story mission. Each chapter consist of 8-11 missions. Different missions give different character's biometrics, Gear Up Kit, Norn Stones(Red, Green, Blue, Pink), STark-branded Blueprint, Component Pack. Normally it costs 4 energy to play story mission, but on hot time it reduces to 3 energy.

Speical Mission

Everyday you will be given 2 entry to complete your special mission and get character biometrics to rank them up. You will also get EXP chip, GUKs, Custom Gear, Obelisk, High obelisk. After completing 2 missions you will be given extra 30 minutes play entry, and you need to activate it to play with 30 minutes mission. Normally this missions costs 6 energy, but on Hot time you will cost 4 energy.

Daily Mission

Daily missions gives you opportunity to earn Gold and some rank 3/4 different types of ISOs. You will be given 2 entry to complete missions. Each missions costs 10 energy. It also has a completion gauge, when you use 6 entry(i.e. 3 days x 2 entries) you will be given some ISOs and Gold.

Villain Siege

Everyday you will be given 3 entry to defeat super villains. You will face different super villain randomly everyday. It has 3 modes: Easy (Boss: Malekith/Bullseye/Green Goblin), Normal (Boss: Destroyer/Kingpin/Hulk), Hard (Ultron/Dr. Octopus/Loki/Yellowjacket)

World Boss

On 16th of March, 2016 Netmarble released Black Order - Part I update. They introduced 3 black order super villains: Proxima Midnight, Black Dwarf and Corvus Glaive. They also introduced Black Anti Matter(aka BAM) and Chaotic Norn Stones(aka CNS).

On 28th of April, 2016 released next update(v2.1.0) Black Order - Part II. They introduced Super Giant, Ebony Maw and Thanos.

Everyday you will be given 5 entry to defeat BO super villains. Different day has different boss starting from Proxima Midnight, Black Dwarf, Corvus Glaive, Super Giant, Ebony Maw, Thanos, Infinity Thanos(Thanos got all 5 kind of gems. So OP).

Arena

Timeline Battle

Everyday you will be given 10 entry to battle with other opponent. But it wouldn't be live, your fight will be with AI. When entry finishes you can recharge new 10 entry point with 125 crystals. Every weekly reset crystals will be rewarded based on your rank on timeline battle. This is the place you can earn crystals, but it is hard to compete. If you lucky and your characters are well developed with good ISO set, decent obelisk you will earn 180-200 crystals. It is too hard to earn 220 Crytals without rechargin with crystals. There currently five league depending on your winning percentage. Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum and Vibranium.

Bronze: Promoted above Top 20%
Silver: Promoted above Top 20%, Demoted below Top 20%
Gold: Promoted above Top 20%, Demoted below Top 20%
Platinum: Promoted above Top 20%, Demoted below Top 20%
Vibranium: Demoted below Top 20%

Co-Op Play

Co-op mission is a live version, where you and other two players can participate to defeat super villains. The most damage dealer gets more percentage and gain more resources. Depending on your VIP level your reward slot will be unlocked. VIP 1 gets 2 slots unlocked. First one always gives random rewards like Booster, Gold, Bios etc. So more your VIP level the more opportunity to gain rewards. There are currently 8 slots based on VIP players.

Low network strength causes disconnections, as well as from server side. As a result you need to play again with same character if that player unable to defeat that boss for the disconnection.

Battleworld

Every weekends or as per the notice there is an event Battleworld. Where you will be given 10/20 entries and 20 entry to change your opponents. There are two Battleworld events one may be normal(You vs AI) and another would be live mode. Live version is currently in beta version, cause sometimes it causes network disconnections. As a result you lose points.

Rewards are given when Battleworld events ends. You will get different rewards like Biometrics/ISO/Comic Card/Obelisk. Battleworld entry costs Stark Branded Blueprint, GUK or Component Pack.

Rewards are distributed in different categories. Rank 1, Rank 2-10, Rank 11-50, Rank 51-100, Rank 10%, Rank 20-30% etc.

Alliance Tournament

If any alliance group gets into Top 16 position, thye will get a chance to compete in Alliance Tournament. In this tournament every participant need to choose opponent and defeat. And the both opponent will play as AI vs AI.

On first day there will no match, this is the day every Top 16 alliance member need to choose their best 3 characters. Players who didn't entered into Top 16 they will get opportunity to cheer for their favorite alliance to earn Gold(and BAM! :/).

From second day match will start 16 team will be divided into 8 match randomly. 3rd day will be 4 match. 4th day 2 match. 5th day will be two match one is for final and another will be for 3rd place holder.
